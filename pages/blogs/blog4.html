There are very few people do not like to travel. But travelling can be so stressful sometime. In this paragraph, I want to share some tips for relaxing and enjoyable travel.

    I travel a lot. Each year I try to leave my comfort zone at-least for a month. In 2018, I have traveled for four months in a row. Those four months was a lesson to me. I have learned from my mistakes. After that tour my travel strategies have changed a lot. Here are some facts that can change anyone's travel experience:

    • While packing it is important to be cautious about what we are packing. Avoiding unnecessary stuffs is a good practice.
    • Less planning is tough to do before visiting a new place, but if you plan less you will be less stressed.
    • Travelling for long period of time can be overwhelming. It is better to relax and take some time for yourself while travelling.
    • I know this is weird, but always try to keep a smartphone with you and make sure the internet is working.

    These are just some of my personal experience and must to do while I am on a tour. We travel for works, enjoyments, lessons and many more. Whatever the reason is but travelling is always special. For a enjoyable journey and to make it better and to avoid unexpected circumstances these are some DON'T of travelling:

    • DON'T use wire transfer or cash.
    • DON'T let your phone roam.
    • DON'T talk politics.
    • Not every country is a free country, and not everyone is tolerant of free speech. It is wise to avoid political gossip while on the road. DON'T spent much time in hotel rooms.

    We all have different opinions, preferences and priorities. Our plan regarding travel can be different from each other, but this strategy guide can help to achieve a harassment free, relaxed travel experience.
