    • About Me

    My name is Md Ashickur Rahman. I am from Rajshahi a city in Bangladesh. For my parent’s jobs, I got a chance to move around at an early age. I have spent my childhood in different cities. But, the longest time of my childhood I have spent in a city called Sylhet. This is a place with a ton of breathtaking sceneries. The city is full of green because of the tea gardens. Overall, it is a beautiful place to live.

    I used to live in a family of four people with my parents and younger brother. It is a pretty small family if I compare it with other families in my country, but, we are a happy family. My parents were kind and helpful all of my life and that is the reason behind who I am now. I’ve always had a friendly relationship with my parents and my younger brother. Now, after living here in Canada for a few months, I realized the importance of having a family-especially when they are loving and kind.

    I have been fascinated by computers since my childhood. The first day I got my computer was a whole new experience for me. I still feel happy when I remember that day. When I got transferred to the school, I learned about programming languages I got excited. It was an amazing moment for me. From that day on, I wanted to learn how-to programming. I tried to learn some of the hardest programming languages without knowing anything about them. I was alone and there was no one to help me with my problems. Then I quit learning programming languages. After coming here, I got to know about the Software Development Program at MITT. I did some research and was happy to see the course outline and decided to get admitted to this program as I always wanted to learn to interact with computers.

    Talking about my hobbies - music and traveling are the things I enjoy the most. I love to play guitar, especially in nature. I love to go solo traveling as well. Music while traveling gives me some kind of joy that makes me calm and wise. Most of the life-changing realization I got from traveling and music. I also love to go hiking, long drive, rock climbing, and cycling.
